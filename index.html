Restaurant Description
A steakhouse, steak house, or chophouse is a restaurant that specializes in steaks and chops. Modern steakhouses can also offer other cuts of meat such as poultry, roast prime rib, veal, and seafood. Chophouses started in London in the 1690s and served individual portions of meat, known as chops.
